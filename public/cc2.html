Internal Parts : Transmission

Transmission Basics

The car transmission is a crucial component responsible for transmitting power from the engine to the wheels, enabling the vehicle to move.

  • Types: Transmissions can be automatic or manual.
  • Components: Key parts include gears, clutch (in manual), torque converter (in automatic), and transmission fluid.
  • Function: It adjusts the torque and speed according to driving conditions.

Transmission Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential to ensure the transmission's longevity and optimal performance.

  • Fluid Check: Regularly check and replace transmission fluid.
  • Service Intervals: Follow manufacturer recommendations for service intervals.
  • Fluid Leaks: Address any fluid leaks promptly to prevent damage.
