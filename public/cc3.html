Internal Parts : Muffler

Muffler Basics

The muffler is a component of the exhaust system designed to reduce the noise emitted by the engine.

  • Function: The muffler dampens the sound waves produced by the engine's exhaust gases.
  • Construction: Typically made of metal and contains chambers and baffles to reduce noise.
  • Importance: Helps in reducing noise pollution and contributes to the overall comfort of the vehicle.
