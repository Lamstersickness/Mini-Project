Internal Parts : Engine

Engine Basics

The car engine is the heart of the vehicle, responsible for generating power and propelling the vehicle forward.

  • Cylinder Block: The main structure of the engine housing cylinders, pistons, and other critical components.
  • Pistons: Move up and down within the cylinders, compressing the air-fuel mixture and generating power.
  • Camshaft: Controls the opening and closing of the engine's valves, allowing air and fuel into the cylinders and expelling exhaust gases.

Engine Components

Various components work together to ensure the engine functions smoothly and efficiently.

  • Spark Plugs: Ignite the air-fuel mixture in the combustion chamber.
  • Fuel Injector: Delivers fuel into the combustion chamber in precise amounts.
  • Oil Pump: Circulates oil to lubricate engine components and reduce friction.
